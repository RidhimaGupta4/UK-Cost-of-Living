Key Finding
London is the only UK region where the average worker cannot cover essential costs. Rent-to-income ratio exceeded 100% in 2023.
Methodology
Affordability Index = (Take-home − Essentials) ÷ Take-home × 100

Essentials = Rent + Groceries + £150 transport

Take-home = Median wage × 72% (after tax/NI)
